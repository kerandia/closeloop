# Product

## Register

product

## Users

B2B sales representatives and team leads managing active pipelines. They use CloseLoop inside a browser during or between customer calls — pulling up deal context, checking AI recommendations, sending follow-up messages. Speed and clarity matter: a rep shouldn't have to think about the interface.

## Product Purpose

CloseLoop is an AI-powered sales intelligence tool. It surfaces deal scores, buyer signals, ghost-risk indicators, and AI-generated next-action recommendations from call transcripts and interaction history. Success looks like a rep who can read a customer's full context and act on it in under 10 seconds.

## Brand Personality

Precise. Decisive. Unobtrusive.

## Anti-references

- Salesforce: bloated, noisy, enterprise gray
- Generic SaaS warm-cream/sage look: soft and friendly is the wrong register for a deal-closing tool
- Startup "delight-first" rounded-everything UI: confuses personality with usefulness

## Design Principles

1. **The tool disappears into the task.** Reps are in flow — every interaction pattern should be instantly recognizable and require zero learning.
2. **Data is the design.** Typography, spacing, and color exist to make information hierarchy legible, not decorative.
3. **Confidence through precision.** Sharp geometry and restrained color signal that the system is reliable — a deal-closing tool should feel like a Bloomberg terminal, not a wellness app.
4. **Orange earns its place.** The solar accent marks the one thing that needs action right now. Use it for primary CTAs and live-state indicators only.
5. **AI should feel like infrastructure.** Flux-green AI elements are present but don't shout — they're a layer below the rep's judgment, not above it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as baseline. Reduced-motion alternative for all animations. Color is never the sole carrier of meaning — all status indicators include text labels alongside color.
